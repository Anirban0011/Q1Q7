Measurement Instruments

  • Goniometer
  1. Task: measures an angle
  2. SI Unit: Radians
  3. Inventor: Gemma Frisius
  • Tachometer
  1. Task: measuring the rotation speed of a shaft or disk
  2. SI Unit: Revolutions per Minute
  3. Inventor: Bryan Donkin
  • Chronometer
  1. Task: used to determine longitude by comparing Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)
  2. SI Unit: Seconds
  3. Inventor: John Harrison
